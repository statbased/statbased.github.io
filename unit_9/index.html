Reflections

Which existing websites out there most inspired your web site design thus far?

I looked at a number of portfolios centered around illustration, art and comics but I modelled it after The Oatmeal in particular. It's a little busy but the target audience is willing to explore because there's a lot of little nooks and crannies of humor and imagery.

Which parts of the design are you most proud of?

I feel I made the experience quite different on each device. There are also a few hints at finesse which I find promising, such as the delayed background transformation on button presses. I'm also proud that this process is becoming kind of like sketching to me.. I finished at a reasonably quick pace without much interruption.

What struggles did you encounter in your design process?

I winged the color scheme and it looks very dated. I also did not plan for ipad, that was done on the fly.

What struggles did you encounter during page development?

I definitely still get confused about nesting. I'm going to work closely on this with my mentor in the remaining sessions.

What did you learn from making your project responsive?

I learned that your page can get really messed up if you don't test it thoroughly and on multiple devices.
